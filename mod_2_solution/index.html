Busy Moms Book Club Schedule

Session 1

BMBC Session 1 runs from January through April. We meet weekly on Monday's at NorthTown Plant Cafe. Books of discussion are "Tomorrow, and Tomorrow, and Tomorrow" by Gabrielle Zevin and "Lessons in Chemistry" by Bonnie Garmus.

Session 2

BMBC Session runs from May through August. We meet weekly on Monday's at Willow Station Park (weather permitting). Books of discussion are "The Measure" by Kikki Erlick and "The Midwife of Auschwitz" by Anna Stuart.

Session 3

BMBC Session runs from September through December. We meet weekly on Monday's at Hollow Creek Bistro. Books of discussion are "It Ends with Us" by Colleen Hoover and "Far From the Tree" by Robin Benway.
